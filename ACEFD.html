ACE Food Drive 2023

My Experience

In 2023, As part of an annual tradition, the ACE leadership board, specifically the ACE Food Drive Team which I was a comittee member of, decided to make the food drive much more interesting that year. We collaborated with multiple organisations such as Buona Vista Youth Network, NUS High School and Nan Hua high school to organise a large scale food drive event. To organise this event, it took alot of logistical planning, from coordinating with other organisation, to presenting and raising donations and packaging the food. On the 25th of June 2023, we set out to distribute the food in the Buona Vista Community were able to help those in need and deliver what they needed.

Reflection

This experience has been a unique and memorable one for me. Through this, I was able to improve my presentation skills as I presented in front of the school to raise awareness of our project and gain donations. Furthermore, I was also able to learn critical skills like logistics, planning, arrangements, leadership and teamwork where they all came hand in hand to allow me and the team to see the project to completion.
